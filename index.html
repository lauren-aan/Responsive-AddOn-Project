Beatshach Bothy


A secluded, traditional granite bothy nestled into side of Ben Rinnes. Located in the heart of Speyside, Beatshach Bothy is the perfect place to enjoy the outdoors, crystal clear skies and, of course, a dram or two.

Speyside is a stunning destination for whisky enthusiasts and nature lovers. With 15 distilleries within 5 miles, Beatshach is ideally place to explore this beautiful corner of the world.

A traditional Bothy built from local granite situated at the foot of Ben Rinnes near Dufftown.
