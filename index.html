About Beatsnach Bothy

A traditional Bothy built from local granite situated at the foot of Ben Rinnes near Dufftown. A comfortable self catering studio layout boasting a wood fired range for heating, kitchenette, double bed, dining area, a separate bathroom and with original windows. The bothy offers beautiful views of the Corryhabbies, is situated in private grounds of 6 acres, you can relax and enjoy the local wildlife. With 15 distilleries within 5 miles, this location is ideal to explore the Malt Whisky Capital.
